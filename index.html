Speak Out Against Drag At Notre Dame

The Department of Film, Television, and Theatre is co-sponsoring a drag show at Notre Dame alongside the Department of Music, the Department of American Studies, the Initiative on Race and Resilience, the Gender Studies Program, and the Institute for Scholarship in the Liberal Arts. The event is expected to take place in the Philbin Studio Theatre at the Debartolo Performing Arts Center (DPAC) on Friday, November 3rd at 7:30 PM.

Protest Information

If you are in the South Bend area or you are a part of the Notre Dame community and would like to join in protest of this event, we will be gathering outside of DPAC beginning at 6:15 pm on Friday, November 3rd. Tickets are available for students to pick up an hour before the event at 6:30 pm.

We strongly encourage those who can to pick up a ticket at the Ticket Office and either enter the blackbox theatre and walk out when the event starts, or not use the ticket (but save a soul from watching the debauchery!)

At 7:30 pm, we will begin praying the sorrowful mysteries of the rosary outside of DPAC.
