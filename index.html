Speak Out Against Drag At Notre Dame

The Department of Film, Television, and Theatre is co-sponsoring a drag show at Notre Dame alongside the Department of Music, the Department of American Studies, the Initiative on Race and Resilience, and the Gender Studies Program. The event is expected to take place in the Philbin Studio Theatre at the Debartolo Performing Arts Center (DPAC) on Friday, November 3rd at 7:30 PM.
